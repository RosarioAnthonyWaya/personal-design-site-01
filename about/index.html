About Rosario Anthony

The thinking
behind the work

First Class physicist. TEDx speaker. Co-founder across two continents. A decade building businesses, brands, and systems across Africa and the UK.

The Arc

Physics. Design.
Business.

Most businesses don't fail because of bad products. They fail because of broken systems.

I graduated First Class in Physics — Best Student, 4.87 CGPA. Not because I wanted to be a scientist, but because I was obsessed with understanding how systems work. What holds things together. Where the real constraint lives. Why things break the way they do.

That obsession led me into design — where I discovered you could apply systems thinking to how people experience a business. I built brands, interfaces, customer journeys, and developed proprietary frameworks — S.E.E. Them™, the 9-Touch Rule™, the Six Feelings Framework — that helped Nigerian SMEs diagnose and fix what wasn't working.

I co-founded Boltzmenn as Africa's SME growth platform. Launched Outhbox UK as a registered UK tech company. Started Design Factory. Curated TEDxGwallameji and spoke at TEDxBauchi. Each step was the same question at a different scale: why isn't this working, and what would actually fix it?
