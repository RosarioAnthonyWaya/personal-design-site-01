Syntyche Language Academy · Nigeria

A school that finally looked like the leader it was

Client
Syntyche Language Academy
Location
Nigeria
Project type
Brand Identity System
Services
Brand StrategyVisual IdentityCommunications ArchitectureBrand Guidelines
The Problem

Syntyche Language Academy was the premium language education destination in its market. The quality of teaching, the results of students, the reputation within the community — all of it spoke to an institution that had earned its position. But the brand didn't reflect any of that.

The Insight

This wasn't a brand problem in the traditional sense. SLA didn't need to be repositioned — it needed to be revealed. The institution already had the substance. The design work was about making the brand worthy of the reputation that already existed.

The Intervention

A full brand identity system was developed — from the logomark to the complete visual language, colour system, and typography. A communications architecture was built to ensure consistency across every student touchpoint. The system was designed to scale as SLA grew.

The Outcome

SLA now presents as the institution it always was. A consistent, professional visual identity that communicates premium quality at every touchpoint — and gives the academy a foundation to build on.
