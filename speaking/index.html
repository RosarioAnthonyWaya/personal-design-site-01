Ready-to-use speaker bio
Rosario Anthony is a growth strategist, systems builder, and TEDx speaker working at the intersection of business performance, design thinking, and African entrepreneurship. Co-founder of Boltzmenn and Outhbox UK, he has spent nearly a decade helping founder-led businesses fix operational leaks and scale with structure. A First Class Physics graduate and MIT UX-trained designer, Rosario brings analytical rigour, creative craft, and commercial intelligence to every stage he stands on.
