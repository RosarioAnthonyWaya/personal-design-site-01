Thinking Systems

Frameworks behind
the work.

Rosario's work combines systems thinking, behavioural psychology, customer experience, and business diagnostics into practical frameworks used across founder-led businesses in Africa and the UK.

—
Six Feelings Framework™
How customer emotion shapes trust, retention, and buying behaviour. A diagnostic tool for understanding why customers leave — and how to make them stay.
—
S.E.E. Them™
A customer experience system built around recognition, engagement, and emotional memory. Designed for businesses that want customers to feel seen, not processed.
—
9-Touch Rule™
A structured operational flow for creating consistent customer experiences across teams and locations. Removes founder dependency from service quality.
